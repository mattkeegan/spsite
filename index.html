Meet SplitPal

SplitPal exists to simplify the process of paying and requesting money from your friends, colleagues and businesses.

It can help you calculate a bill split, keep track of IOUs and facilitate instant payment. For convenience, everything is accessible from your iPhone, iPad or Android device and all transactions can be settled via MasterCard, Visa or American Express debit and credit cards.

So, whether you're sharing a ski vacation, splitting rent with roommates, or fixing up a bill at lunch, join the crowd in making money hassles a thing of the past!

Just a couple of the features

Lightning fast cash transfers

Most payments are transferred to your bank within 60mins; and splitting the cost of a meal, holiday or household bill has never been so easy with our bill splitter!

Single click payments / requests

Use SplitPal with anyone, even if they don’t have the app. Simply make or request a payment and we'll take care of the rest. IOU's are now a thing of the past!

Credit card capability

Making a payment via your bank debit card is always free with SplitPal, but now you now can pay back your friends or bills using any major credit card, including Mastercard, Visa or American Express.

We take security seriously

Using the latest in cutting edge technology, SplitPal draws on independent payment processor Stripe to securely store your payment details and process your transaction.

Stripe is trusted by some of the worlds largest companies, including Virgin, Twitter and KickStarter.

What happens when I send a request to my friends for payment?

Your friend will receive a message letting them know that they have an IOU with you. If they have the SplitPal app they can simply hit "pay" when they receive the notification. If not, they can download the app or make a once off payment by following the secure link in our SMS to entering their payment details.

What happens if I don't get paid?

Friends can sometimes be forgetful. To help make sure you get paid we'll send them a nice reminder after 3 and 7 days to follow-up for you. With us taking all the heat, you get paid back without the awkward text or phone call.

How do I know my payment and bank details are safe?

When you first enter your credit card details (or need to enter a new card) we will send you a 128-bit encrypted HTTPS link via SMS or in app link. Importantly though, we do not store your credit card number on our servers. All payment processing is handled securely via Stripe (which is the same service used by Twitter, Virgin, Xero and Kickstarter - to name a few).

Do payments happen instantly?

Time is money, so whenever you make a payment we'll instantly process and send it. Most transfers are credited within 1-4hrs. However, sometimes there are things outside our control which slow things down. For example, some banks require at least a 48hr holding period before payments to new accounts are released. In addition, some banks will require a holding period until your payment is released to us. These can slow things down, but we'll always process your transaction and release it as soon as possible :)

Is there a limit to how much I can split or send?

For security purposes, everyone starts out with a rolling weekly limit of $299.99. Once you've verified your identity, your weekly rolling limit will increase to $2999.99.
